About Patna

Chennai, the capital city of Bihar, is a vibrant metropolis renowned for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and bustling streets. From stunning Ghats to magnificent temples, Patna offers a diverse range of experiences for tourists.

Popular Attractions

Marina Beach

Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world, stretching along the Bay of Bengal. Visitors can enjoy activities like horse riding, beach volleyball, and sampling local street food.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, Kapaleeshwarar Temple is an ancient Hindu temple known for its Dravidian architecture and vibrant festivals. Visitors can witness traditional rituals and marvel at the intricate carvings.
